Vortex Effect

The VortexEffect component applies two forces to an object to simulate a spinning vortex. - An attractive linear force pulls objects towards the center of the disc. - A perpendicular linear force twists objects around the center of the disc.

The same interpolation scheme as PointForceEffect is used by VortexEffect to compute the inward strength and twist strength.

Application Modes

VortexEffect is only expected to work as a Region effect. All other application modes are undefined.
